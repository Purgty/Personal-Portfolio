## OCI AI Foundations Associate Certification

This certification validates foundational knowledge in **Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI)** Artificial Intelligence services and essential machine-learning concepts.

It demonstrates competency in:

- Core cloud + AI concepts  
- OCI AI/ML services and their use cases  
- Data preparation basics  
- Fundamentals of generative AI  
- Responsible and secure AI practices
