## OCI Generative AI Professional Certification

This certification validates advanced proficiency in **Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI)** and the development, deployment, and optimization of **Generative AI solutions** using OCI's model ecosystem and AI services.

It certifies expertise in:

- Generative AI development workflows  
- Custom model training and tuning  
- AI infrastructure on OCI  
- Vector databases and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)  
- Governance, security, and responsible AI practices  

### Issuing Authority  
Oracle
